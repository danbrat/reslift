poetree


nah, you dont get it
i wanna have your baby.
throw me in the air and splatter
me with stars your graham-cookies
and milk. spit on me, send me
somewhere then call me back.
you made me late for work and i
dont even know you anymore.
the stewardess came on the PA
a new destination
a red alarm went off
the plane was crashing
we hit the water
I woke up and there you were
curled up next to me like a cat
dont even know you anymore.
